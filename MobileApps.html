Bouba District Special

Bouba District! is a competitive rhythm-based game that combines action and music mechanics. Face hordes of adorable yet dangerous creatures by following the beat and executing precise key sequences. It blends tower defense mechanics with rhythm games. The main objective is to prevent the creatures from invading the city.

Synopsis

In this special version, we created an exclusive mobile level that implements swipes and the inertia sensor (accelerometer). This way, we achieve a more dynamic experience with unique mechanics and gameplay designed exclusively for mobile devices.

The accelerometer is represented by a vertical progress bar on the left side of the interface. As the player advances and defeats Boubas, the bar fills up. Once full, it unlocks the accelerometer function, allowing the player to shake the device to trigger a shock wave, eliminating all Boubas on the screen.

  • Swipes
  • Accelerometer
  • Ranking System

Merge Down

This project aims to apply mobile app development with Flutter by creating a casual game. The application takes inspiration from games like "2048" and "Tetris", adapting their core mechanics to offer a fresh and dynamic experience for all users.

The game features a board where columns must be filled with generated numbers. The player's goal is to match tiles with the same numbers to reach the highest possible value before filling all available spaces. Additionally, the game interface includes a score counter to keep the player informed of their progress and a move counter to track the number of numbers that have been moved.

Inspiration

We decided to recreate the 2048 video game for several reasons. First, its simplicity allowed us to achieve satisfying results even with limited experience. Additionally, its minimalist and visually appealing design makes it accessible and enjoyable for all types of players. Finally, its high replay value and addictive mechanics create an immersive experience, making time fly with each number combination.
