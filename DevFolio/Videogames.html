Bouba District

Bouba District! is a competitive rhythm-based game that combines action and music mechanics. Face hordes of adorable yet dangerous creatures by following the beat and executing precise key sequences. It blends tower defense mechanics with rhythm games. The main objective is to prevent the creatures from invading the city.

Competition in Bouba District is fierce, with a global ranking active at all times. Only the top three players will stand out, displaying their name and skin for everyone to see. Pull off impressive combos and take down the fearsome Bouba bosses to reach the top!

Synopsis

A failed experiment has given rise to "The Boubas", slimy creatures determined to conquer everything in their path. Kiki, the young music prodigy, along with his trendy boy band, must use his rhythmic skills to face these mutants and save the city.

Bouba District is bigger than we thought... With three completely different locations, our artists have a vast world to explore and save with the power of music. These locations are: Harmonia, Synthesia, and Soulwood.

What you need to know

  • Developed in Unity
  • Ranking System
  • 6 Skins Available
  • 3 Unique Environments
  • Competitive

The Last Candle

The Last Candle falls within the horror genre and combines escape room elements with puzzle-solving, creating an atmosphere of tension and mystery. The core gameplay pits two players against each other, each with opposing objectives to win the match. It is published on Itch.io in its local version, while the downloadable file includes an online version with communication via WebSockets and API REST.

Synopsis

The game's plot revolves around two protagonists: an exorcist and a powerful demon. The demon has taken control of a house, which is now cursed, and the exorcist has been hired to cleanse it on behalf of a client. However, upon entering, the exorcist realizes that they are trapped inside. To survive, he must exorcise the demon by completing a ritual that will allow them to escape alive.

What you need to know

  • WebSockets & API REST
  • 2D & Animations
  • 4 Skins Avaible
